Channel 1: Facebook Groups

⭐ Most Recommended

Find the Right Groups

  • Search keywords: Romance Books, Werewolf Romance, Dark Romance, Billionaire Romance, Mafia Romance, Web Fiction, Free Books, Book Recommendations
  • Prioritize groups with 500+ members and recent activity
  • Observe group rules after joining — some groups ban promotions, others allow them

Posting Tips

  1. No hard selling — Say "I'm reading this book" instead of "Come download now"
  2. Share real reading experiences — "Chapter 47 made me cry my eyes out, the male lead is such a jerk but I'm so hooked"
  3. Use screenshots as bait — Share censored cliffhanger screenshots to spark curiosity
  4. Ask questions for engagement — "Has anyone read this? How's the ending?"
  5. Series posts — Spread posts about the same book over several days, leaving cliffhangers each time

Post Templates

📖 I just found this werewolf romance and I can NOT put it down... The alpha rejected his mate but now he's crawling back?? 😭🔥 Chapters 1-30 are FREE on NovelFlow if anyone wants to binge this weekend!
[Your Referral Link]

Channel 2: Facebook Public Pages

Growing Channel

Posting Strategy

  • Post book reviews and recommendations on your own page
  • Use eye-catching cover images (novel screenshots + emotional copy)
  • Your copy needs a hook: "This book ruined my sleep schedule and I'm not even mad"

Comment Section Engagement

  • Comment on posts from similar book pages
  • "I read something similar on NovelFlow, the werewolf rejection trope goes CRAZY in this one"
  • Don't spam — naturally blend into conversations

Core Promotion Principles

✅ Do This

  • Recommend books you've actually read — Authenticity is your biggest selling point
  • Focus on one genre to start — Start with Werewolf Romance and dominate that niche
  • Post consistently — 2-3 posts daily is better than 10 posts one day then disappearing
  • Create suspense — "I was blown away by Chapter 30" is 100x more effective than "This book is great"
  • Reply to every comment — More engagement = higher post visibility
  • Cover multiple accounts and groups — Posting in 5 groups doubles your reach

❌ Don't Do This

  • Don't drop links immediately like a bot
  • Don't hard-sell in groups that ban promotions — you'll get kicked out
  • Don't copy-paste identical content across different groups — it'll be flagged as spam
  • Don't exaggerate earnings — nobody believes "easy $10K+ a month"
  • Don't post once and disappear — consistent exposure drives conversions
📊

Tips to Maximize Earnings

Boost Conversion Rates

  1. Guide users to register, not just download — Make sure users register through your link so all their future spending counts for you
  2. Recommend books with plenty of free chapters — Once readers get hooked on free content, they'll naturally upgrade to VIP
  3. Recommend ongoing series — Readers following ongoing stories are more likely to maintain subscriptions
  4. Teach users how to use the app — Some users don't know how to register/recharge; help them out

Improve Retention Rates

  1. Keep recommending new books — Give your readers constant fresh content
  2. Build your own reader group — Add recommended readers to a group and broadcast new book recommendations
  3. Pay attention to reader feedback — Recommend more of the genres they love
