🎯 KOC Promotion Guide

How to Promote Books

A step-by-step guide to sharing books and earning rewards through the NovelFlow KOC program.

Getting Started
1
Submit Your Book Recommendation
Go to the Submit page and fill in the book details you want to recommend. Include the book name and your preferred promotion method.
2
Wait for Your Referral Link
Our system will create your unique referral link and promo code. This usually takes 3 minutes or less.
3
Check the Recommendation Wall
Visit the Recommendation Wall to find your unique referral link, code, and ready-to-use promo copy. Click to expand your user card.
4
Share on Social Platforms
Copy your link, code, and promo text. Share them on Facebook Groups, Discord, TikTok, or any platform where readers gather.
5
Earn Rewards!
When new users sign up through your referral link, you'll earn rewards! Keep track of your referrals and enjoy the benefits.
